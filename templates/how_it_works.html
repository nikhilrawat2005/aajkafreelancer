How It Works

  1. Sign up with your college email
    Create an account using your college email ID to ensure all users belong to the same campus community.
  2. List or explore skills
    Students can offer their skills as services, while others can browse available workers and find the right person for their needs.
  3. Chat before hiring
    Use the built-in chat to discuss requirements, clarify expectations, and decide if the worker is the right fit.
  4. Send a hire request
    Once satisfied, send a hire request. The worker can accept or reject the request based on availability.
  5. Track payments and earnings
    Workers can monitor completed jobs, payment records, and total earnings through their payment dashboard.
